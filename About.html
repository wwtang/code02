About us

Euro So.Cap. is the leader in the field of 100% natural Remy hair extension manufacturing. Euro So.Cap. has chosen to use only and exclusively pure Indian hair since its characteristics and structural properties perfectly complement European hair. With over 40 years of professionalism and experience, Euro So.Cap Italia has held a distinguished presence in the Hair Extension field. It has established itself as a high quality brand. Our new Euro So.Cap USA office will allow us to service all of our clients and commercial partners better and provide them with speedier service, greater ease and in all our five hair extension techniques:

1. Hair Extensions with Keratin bonding
2. Sticker Hair Extensions with bi-adhesive tape
3. Weft Hair Extensions
4. Clip-on Hair Extensions
5. Beauty full, Euro So.Cap.'s new and exclusive system of hair extensions formed by a band of flexible keratin bonding and hair extension clips

Euro So.Cap now offers professional training and certification for hair extensions. Our training programs include four different packages:

1. GOLD Certification: Learn how to apply hair extensions with the latest technology of Cold Fusion Ultrasound. This machine is exclusive to Euro So.Cap. The course includes: A Cold Fusion machine, a color ring in natural hair, a hair extension remover, assembly pincers, 50 strands of natural hair extensions with keratin bonding in 20 " in the color of your preference
See: GOLD Certification

2. SILVER Certification : Learn our classic hot-system for hair extension application. The course includes: Kit 2005, a color ring in natural hair, a hair extension remover, assembly and disassembly pincers, scalp protecting disks, 50 strands in natural hair extension with keratin bonding 20" in the color of your preference

3. STAR Certification : Learn our sticker hair extension application. This course includes: Assembly pinchers, five sticker hair tape rolls, one hair extension remover, one color ring, five packages of sticker hair extensions, double sided tape strips, and 18 strands (3 bags) of sticker hair extensions in 20-22” in the color of your preference
See: STAR Certification

4. SILVER PLUS certification : This certification includes our classic hot system (Ref.: 2) , as well as our Sticker Hair Extension application technique (Ref.: 3). The course includes: the SILVER and STAR certification kits.

For more information on this courses and training availability please Contact Us at our offices in the US.
